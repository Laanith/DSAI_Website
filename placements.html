Mehta Family School of DS&AI

Placement

The School of DS&AI attracts talented individuals to be a part of a dynamic and thriving academic community. The designed course curriculum imparts knowledge ensuring an attitude to take up challenging tasks in the professional career. We are confident that our students will make significant contributions to the field of DS&AI.
We invite recruiters and organizations to harness the potential of our talented students and witness their transformative impact on the industry. You can gather more information about placement proedures at IIT Guwahati from the Center for Career Development (CCD) or our School's Faculty-in-Charge Career Development.
